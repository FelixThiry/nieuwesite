Since the summer of 2016 Felix has started to dabble with music production under the moniker
of 'Papi Mandarin'. His music can be described as trap instrumentals with dreamy undertones (Papi Jochen, Papi Fingers Blue) or hard percussion with dark melodies (Papi Imhotep).

Papi has remixed three already existing tracks to fit his musical dream world landscape. One of the exceptions being Papi No Heart which he has transformed into a more dark and eerie sound that 21 Savage and Metro Boomin aspired to create.

With the help of his producer friends such as Hush, Doctrine, Nightshop and Oldboi, Papi is working to evolve his style and create contemplative and melancholic beats for rappers to work with or as stand alone tracks.
